All Things New

by Josh Boepple

Coming to God with all of yourself, even when it seems hard or somewhat impossible.
True humility is agreeing with what God wants in your life.

Episode 1

God wants all of us and He will make us whole, we have to humble ourselves and come to Him and fully give ourselves to Him.


Episode 2

God is always there, we just have to be able to listen.


Episode 3

When you begin to feel the pressure of this world trying to suffocate you, remember it's not your job to run your own life in the first place. Rest assured knowing that He is working everything out for your good. The pressure is on Him! He can handle it!
